Our History

2001 We started very small scale but we've kept the same standard of quality and professionalism to this very day.

Since then we've grown into a larger business franchise than anticipated but we still dedicate to serve our customers in the best of our capabilities. We have built our business to give our customers the full package; delicious food and amazing customer care.

We pride ourselves in serving the best food in the city and anyone is welcome to challenge that fact. The future is only brighter from here on and we invite you to join our family and lighten up your life.
